Introduction

This guideline defines the terms of use for all UTAU voicebanks and distributed characters on this site.

By downloading the voicebank, you are considered to have agreed to these terms.

Usage

These voicebanks may be used for music production, video uploads, tuning, and other works using UTAU.

Use with OpenUtau is also permitted; however, the creator has not tested these voicebank with OpenUtau and cannot guarantee its performance or audio output.

The recommended environment is the original UTAU version by Ameya/Ayame.

Personal modifications and adjustments to the oto.ini or voicebank files are allowed. Please note that redistribution of modified voicebank files is generally prohibited.

If you wish to share a modified oto.ini, please contact the creator in advance.

Derivative works related to these voicebank are allowed, except for the prohibited uses listed below.

Prohibited Uses

The following uses are prohibited:

  • Redistribution or unauthorized uploading of these voicebank files
  • Claiming these voicebanks as your own work
  • Use for AI training, AI-generated content, or the creation of voice models using these voicebanks
  • Using these voicebanks as the voice of a character different from the distributed character
  • Romantic expressions or pairings involving these distributed characters
  • Sexual or explicit content (R18 material)
  • Gore, graphic violence, or excessively cruel expressions (G18 material)
  • Use for political or religious activities
    ※ Expressions involving general seasonal events or cultural events are permitted.
  • Commercial use of these distributed characters’ illustration artwork (merchandise production, sales, etc.)
  • Other uses or expressions that significantly damage the image of these voicebanks or characters, or that the creator considers inappropriate

Commercial Use

Any use involving monetary gain, including monetization, requires prior consultation.

※ Commercial use of the distributed character’s illustration artwork is prohibited.

Credits

Credit is optional. If you choose to include credits, please list the voicebank name.
